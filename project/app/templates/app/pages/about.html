About

My name is David Binetti, I'm a parent of two West Ada students, and I know that change — even change for the better — is really, really hard. So I started this site to support the Board and give them confidence to make masks optional.

Personally, I'm an entrepreneur who has started a few companies and these days I consult for Fortune 500 companies. But more important than who I am is what I am not: specifically, I am not political. I'm a registered independent, I don't have any axes to grind, and I have precisely zero history with the current Board (aside from applying for the open spot in Zone 4 and signing up as a Substitute Teacher!) This is totally free, I'm keeping your information private, and when our mission is complete the entire site will be completely and permanently deleted.

I just want our schools filled with smiles. That's all I want. If that's all you want, then join us.
